01 — The Vision

Heritage & Craft

Design Philosophy

Rooted in the 11-century lineage of Prince Federico, uniforms prioritize structure, bespoke fit, and aristocratic presence. We utilize Milanese silhouettes and fine linen-wool blends to honor Italian noble tailoring.

Local heritage is expressed through traditional embroidery (Alamari), the integration of the Fez/Tarbouch, and breathable linen fabrics suited for the Marrakech light.

"The material expression of a dynasty in motion."

02 — Attire Pillars

The Principles of Dress

Aristocratic

Every piece of clothing conveys a sense of belonging to an 11-century lineage, never loud but always certain[cite: 3].

Sensorial

Fabrics are chosen for their tactile quality—linen, silk, and velvet—designed to interact with the unique Marrakech light[cite: 3].

Timeless

Avoiding trend-based "luxury," the uniforms focus on silhouettes that could exist in 1920 or 2025[cite: 3].

Culinary Team

Gastronomic Nobility

Our kitchen staff embodies precision. The uniform features high-collar white linen with the gold crowned monogram prominently displayed on the chest[cite: 3]. The assistant wears a complementary design, ensuring a cohesive aesthetic[cite: 3].

Security & Protection

Guardians of Leonessa

A regal presence at the gates. The security uniform utilizes 'Leonessa Night' navy wool with traditional gold-threaded Alamari closures and shoulder braiding[cite: 3]. Completed with a structured velvet Fez[cite: 3].

Estate Maintenance

Stewards of the Earth

Designed for the golden light of the Palmeraie. Heavy cotton and linen in 'Marrakech Rose' and 'Ivory' tones allow for movement[cite: 3]. Refined straw hats with the gold wordmark provide functional protection[cite: 3].
